## 1. Sales department ✅ (FINAL)

### Admin / Department Manager (Sales)

1. Total Leads — All leads in system

2. Conversion Rate — Lead to sale percentage

3. Monthly Revenue — Revenue from cracked leads

4. Total Won Deals — Deals closed this month (company-wide)

### Unit Head (Sales Unit)

1. Unit Leads — Leads assigned to unit

2. Unit Conversion Rate — Unit-specific conversion

3. Unit Revenue — Revenue generated by unit

4. Unit Won Deals — Deals closed this month (unit-wide)

### Team Lead (Sales Team)

1. Team Leads — Leads assigned to team

2. Team Conversion Rate — Team conversion percentage

3. Team Revenue — Revenue generated by team

4. Team Won Deals — Deals closed this month (team-wide)

### Senior/Junior Sales Rep (Employee)

1. My Assigned Leads — Personal lead count

2. My Conversion Rate — Personal conversion percentage

3. My Commission Earned — Personal commission this month

4. My Won Deals — Deals closed this month (personal)
